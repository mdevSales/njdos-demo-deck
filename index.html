Chapter Two

The recruiter
meets Paul.

Stages 4 through 7 — where the platform earns its keep. AI does the busywork; the recruiter does the human work.

NJ DOS Recruiter
Talent Team
STAGE 04

The recruiter workspace.

What the recruiter sees

Monday morning: one queue, applications grouped by role, sorted by qualification fit. The top of the queue is the highest-probability hire — not the newest application.

Where AI shows up

Every candidate arrives pre-scored against the job requisition. The recruiter's time goes to the top ten, not to sorting the bottom thirty.

NJ DOS Recruiter
Talent Team
STAGE 05

Paul's candidate record.

What the recruiter sees

A plain-English AI summary at the top of Paul's record: experience, skill match, gaps. Fifteen minutes of resume-reading collapses to three seconds of context.

Where AI shows up

Conversational qualification match — the recruiter can ask "which requirements does Paul meet?" and get a per-requirement breakdown, each answer citing the exact line from the resume. Decision support, not decision replacement.

NJ DOS Recruiter
Talent Team
STAGE 06

The candidate pipeline.

What the recruiter sees

A visible pipeline: Submitted → In Review → Interview → Offer → Accepted → Hired. Moving Paul forward is one click — and the system knows what happens next.

Automation behind each stage

Move to In Review? The hiring manager gets the AI summary. Move to Interview? Scheduling kicks in. Move to Offer? Legal is notified. Every handoff is instrumented.

Hiring Manager
Engineering Lead
STAGE 07

Structured interview feedback.

What the hiring manager sees

An evaluation form with scores per interviewer, per competency — technical depth, communication, mission fit. Everyone measured against the same rubric. Comparable. Auditable. Defensible.

Where AI shows up

Interview synthesis: consensus themes, dissenting concerns, hiring recommendation. Grounds the go/no-go conversation in a shared picture — efficiency and fairness in the same motion.

STAGE 09 Hero Moment

Candidate → employee, in one click.

What the recruiter sees

Paul accepts. A "Convert to Employee" step appears. Three-step guided flow: confirm details (pre-filled), pick an onboarding playbook (engineering, sales, or standard), review and convert.

The gap that closes to zero

One platform, not four.

  • Consolidation. Careers, ATS, offer generation, HR onboarding on one data model and one security model.
  • AI grounded in your data. Every AI output cites its source. Nothing hallucinated, everything reviewable.
  • Extensible. The same platform already powers your policy workflows. Talent joins as another tenant, not another silo.
  • Compliance-ready. Structured evaluations, versioned offer PDFs, complete audit trail from application to hire.
For the Head of HR

Recruiters back on human work.

  • Fifteen minutes per resume, reclaimed. AI does the first pass. Your team spends time on candidates, not on triage.
  • Fairness built in. Structured evaluations and AI-cited fit summaries reduce interviewer bias and support defensible decisions.
  • Candidate experience. The citizen sees a modern, transparent process — critical for the state's brand as an employer.
  • Day-one ready. Zero-gap conversion means laptop, badge, and training are prepped before the new hire walks in.
